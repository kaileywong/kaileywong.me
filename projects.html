STEM From Scratch

Education, STEM

STEM From Scratch is a student-run organization that aims to provide equal access to STEM opportunities for students around the world. Working with the founder of STEM From Scratch, as the executive director, I developed the project from a website into an organization with fiscal sponsorship from a 501(c)(3) nonprofit. I developed an organizational structure for the team, creating a leadership team and starting 5 new departments. I also established branding guidelines, redesigned the website and social media, expanded social media presence, initiated the newsletter project, created an onboarding system for new members, and developed the blog.
